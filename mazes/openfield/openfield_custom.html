Assembly Instructions

1. Follow the instructions to create a basic open field, creating the starting size of your custom open field.

2. To add alcoves, use right-angle junctions (with additional floor and wall panels).


3. To add separating walls, use 3-way junctions instead of inserts.


4. To create a multiple connected open fields, use a 4-way junction.


Enjoy your custom open field!
