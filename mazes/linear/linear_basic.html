Assembly Instructions

1. Pick a size for your linear track, with a length of *N* number of panels (each panel == 8 cm).

2. Print out all parts listed in the sidebar based on the size of your linear track.
(how to 3D print)

3. Connect all floor panels of the track.

4. Attach straight junctions to the sides of the track (but not the corner junctions yet).

5. Slide wall panels in between the junctions on the sides of the track (but not the corner walls yet).

6. Holding a right-angle junction in place on one corner of the track, slide the final corner wall panels into place. Repeat for all corners.

The linear track is now complete!

Assembled linear track

Parts

N x square floor panels

(2N)+2 x wall panels

4 x 2-way junctions (right angle)

2(N-1) x 2-way junctions (straight)
